Chip Butty

Description

The evergreen British classic. It ain't classy, it ain't pretty but it's a dependable carbo-bomb, easily customised through addition of sauces

Ingredients

  • Bread
    • No artisan sourdough here - the cheapest, nastiest pre-sliced supermarket loaf you can get your hands on!
  • Butter
    • Who are we kidding, it's gotta be a margarine spread!
  • Chips
    • Oven chips will do, but if you're feeling naughty, why not pop to the chippy!
  • Sauces
    • Optional of course but we all have a favourite!

Steps

  1. Take a couple of slices of bread
  2. Liberally spread them with butter/marge
  3. If using oven chips, cook as per instructions on bag
  4. Pile chips on one slice as high as you dare then make a sandwich with the other slice
