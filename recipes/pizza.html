Pizza

Description

This pizza is an approximation of the thin crust style pizza preferred in the South of Italy

If you follow this recipe, I promise you'll end up with an authentic and delicious pizza!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Yeast
  • Quality tinned tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, water, salt, yeast and olive oil in a large bowl
  2. Knead the dough for ten minutes until smooth
  3. cover with a cloth and leave to rise
  4. spread the dough into a pizza shape
  5. add the topping ingredients
  6. cook in an extremely hot oven
