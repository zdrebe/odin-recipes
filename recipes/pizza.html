Pizza

Description

The now ubiquitous South Italian dish is easy to prepare at home from scratch.

If you follow this recipe, I promise you'll end up with an authentic and delicious pizza!

Ingredients

  • Flour
    • I recommend Italian 00 Strong flour for this recipe
  • Water
    • I recommend lukewarm water
  • Salt
    • Just a pinch will do!
  • Olive Oil
    • Regular will do just fine. No need to waste extra virgin!
  • Yeast
    • Dried yeast is fine but many bakeries will provide you with fresh if asked
  • Quality tinned tomatoes
    • I recommend the brand Bianco DiNapoli
  • Mozzarella
    • The fresher the better!
  • Basil
    • Your local greengrocer can order it for you if you can't find any.

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, water, salt, yeast and olive oil in a large bowl
  2. Knead the dough for ten minutes until smooth
  3. cover with a cloth and leave to rise
  4. spread the dough into a pizza shape
  5. add the topping ingredients
  6. cook in an extremely hot oven
