Spanish Omelette

Description

Ingredients

  • Eggs
    • The amount will depend on the size of the omelette. Probably between 4 and 6.
  • Potatoes
    • Peeled and thinly sliced
  • Oil
    • Olive oil will give you a healthy and tasty tortilla but sunflower oil is fine and much cheaper

Steps

  1. Heat a large amount of oil to a medium heat in a deep frying pan.
  2. Add the sliced and peeled potatoes and cook slowly until soft
  3. Drain the oil
  4. Beat the eggs
  5. add to the potatoes, ensuring they are mixed well
  6. flip the omelette with the help of a plate
  7. cook on a mlow-medium heat until done

ATTENTION! The idea is to cook the potatoes gently until soft - NOT to fry to a crisp!
